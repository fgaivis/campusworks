CHATTANOOGA - 10TH ST. STUDENT HOUSING

CampusWorks Company, was awarded the contract to build, Douglas Heights, a new 691 bed, purpose built student housing community serving the student population at The University of Tennessee at Chattanooga.


930 NOMO PROJECT AT THE
COLLEGE OF CHARLESTON

CampusWorks Development, a Charlotte based student housing developer and builder announced construction on a ground breaking development for a 430 bed off-campus student housing community serving the College of Charleston. The project, 930 NoMo, is a joint venture partnership with Kayne Anderson Real Estate Advisors and Kaplan Residential. The senior lender is PNC Real Estate and the project is expected to be delivered for the fall 2015 academic school year.
