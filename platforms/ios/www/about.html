Who we are

CampusWorks is a nationally recognized developer and builder of student housing apartment communities. Since its founding in 1999, CampusWorks has experienced extensive growth, with a combined student housing portfolio exceeding 13,500 beds in Acquisition, Development & Construction. Additionally CampusWorks has a joint venture ownership interest in over 7,200 student housing beds in 15 university markets in excess of $500 million.

Where We Are

CampusWorks provides its specialized development and full service general contracting services from its headquarters based outside of Charlotte, North Carolina.

Industry Presence

CampusWorks and its joint venture partners are owners, managers, builders, and developers of purpose built student housing apartment communities throughout the U.S. and focus on the acquisition, design, finance, development, construction, management and leasing of student housing properties.

How We Partner

CampusWorks believes in a true partnership. We strive to understand not only the scope of development and construction but the direction of the overall real estate strategy. We hope to align our interests by participating in projects that make sense with our company's goals. By doing so we provide a vehicle in securing equity and debt relationships with sponsorship capabilities. Additionally as a vertically integrated developer our construction division can provide a completion guarantee and we have the appetite to be a partner in all acquisition and development projects.
